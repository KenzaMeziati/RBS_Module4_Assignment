# RBS_Module4_Assignment
Biweekly COVID-19 Progress Report : Modeling the Evolution and Policy Proposals for Effective National Health Response - Italy/Lazio Use Case



## Purpose and Objectives 
The primary goal of this sutdy is to analyse the biweekly evolution of the COVID-19 pandemic in Italy, 
with a specific focus on the Lazio region. The analysis will be based on available datasets, including 
regional and provincial COVID-19 case reports, population demographics, and municipal data. The 
report aims to: 
● Track and visualize the spread of COVID-19 at the national and regional levels. 
● Identify trends in hospitalizations, intensive care admissions, and recovery rates. 
● Develop predictive models to estimate future cases and assess healthcare system 
preparedness. 
● Provide data-driven policy recommendations to mitigate the impact of the pandemic. 

## Data Sources and Methodology 
The analysis presented in this report relies on multiple datasets, including: 
● Italian Municipalities Dataset: Provides demographic and geographic data. 
● COVID-19 Regional Dataset: Contains daily updates on cases, hospitalizations, intensive care 
patients, recoveries, and deaths at the regional level. 
● COVID-19 Provincial Dataset: Offers a more granular view of case distribution at the provincial 
level. 
● Municipality Altitude Dataset: Includes geographic altitude information, which may help 
assess correlations between location and infection trends. 
Our approach involves data cleaning, visualization, and predictive modelling using statistical and 
machine learning techniques. The findings will be presented through charts, graphs, and structured 
summaries.
